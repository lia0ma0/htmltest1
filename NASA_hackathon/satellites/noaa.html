NOAA-20-SATELLITE:

NOAA-20 Satellite, also known as the Joint Polar Satellite System-1 (JPSS-1) was launched on November 18, 2017. It is the first of the United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's latest generation of U.S. polar-orbiting, non-geosynchronous, environmental satellites. The mission of this satellite is for monitoring the environment and also predicting severe weather that might happen on Earth.
