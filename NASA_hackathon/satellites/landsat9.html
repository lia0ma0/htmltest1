LANDSAT9:

Landsat 9, launched on September 27, 2021, is the latest satellite serving for the Landsat program. Landsat 9 was sent to repeat global observations for monitoring, understanding and managing Earthâ€™s natural resources needed to sustain human life. Just like Landsat 8, Landsat 9 has a higher image capacity, allowing more data being added to the Landsat Global Archive. Landsat 9 also replaced Landsat 7 and took its place in orbit. The images in our database that are sent by Landsat 9 are all mainly captured through the lenses of the Operational Land Imager as well.
