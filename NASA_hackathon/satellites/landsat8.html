LANDSAT8:

Landsat 8, launched on February 11, 2013, was sent to space to continue the program of Earth imagery acquisition that the US has been implementing since 1972 with the help of the Landsat series and other satellites in the Earth Observation System. Landsat 8 contains two science instruments which are the Operational Land Imager (OLI) and the Thermal Infrared Sensor (TIRS). The images in our database that are sent by Landsat 8 are all mainly captured through the lenses of the Operational Land Imager.
